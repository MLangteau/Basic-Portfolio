About Me

Ms. Langteau is earning her Certification from the University of Central Florida's (UCF) Coding Bootcamp for Web Design and Development in July 2017. She has utilized the following in the six-month bootcamp and in projects that are in her GitHub Repositories: Ajax, Bootstrap, Cascading Style Sheets (CSS3), Express, GitBash, GitHub, HTML5, JavaScript, jQuery, MongoDB , MySQL, Node.js, Object Relation Mapping (ORM), Sequelize, and Sublime Text3.a dynamic full stack programmer.

In the past, she was a Consultant working as a senior software engineer and programmer analyst at many different companies, such as Maryland National Bank, AT&T, NASA, Coca-Cola Enterprises East, and the Health and Human Services.

Ms. Langteau enjoys swimming, reading, and singing, not necessarily at the same time. Since moving to a sunny location, she also loves to scuba dive, and travel.
